Thalapathy Vijay

Actor | Singer | Philanthropist

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, popularly known as Thalapathy Vijay, is a leading actor in the Tamil film industry. Known for his charismatic screen presence and powerful performances, he has a massive fan following across India and beyond.

Notable movies include: *Mersal*, *Master*, *Bigil*, *Thuppakki*, and *Leo*.
