Understanding the Causes and Effects of Pollution

What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful materials into the environment. These harmful materials are called pollutants. Pollution can take the form of chemical substances or energy, such as noise, heat, or light.

Types of Pollution

  • Air Pollution
  • Water Pollution
  • Soil Pollution
  • Noise Pollution

How Can We Help?

We can reduce pollution by using cleaner energy sources, recycling, reducing waste, and spreading awareness. Everyone has a role to play in protecting our environment.
