Indian Police Service (IPS)

The Indian Police Service (IPS) is one of the three All India Services of the Government of India. IPS officers serve in key positions in the police forces, both at the state and central levels.

Roles and Responsibilities

IPS officers handle law and order, crime prevention, traffic control, and counterterrorism. They also manage intelligence and security organizations like CBI, IB, and RAW.

How to become an IPS Officer?

Aspiring candidates must clear the UPSC Civil Services Examination, which includes Prelims, Mains, and an Interview.
